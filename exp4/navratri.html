NAVRATRI

Navratri is a common topic essay writing topic for students of lower primary classes. Kids love festivals and the fervour it brings. So, writing an essay in English on Navratri for kids in classes 1, 2 and 3 is an interesting assignment. Navratri is a nine-day long festival, which means children get to enjoy all the festivities, dancing, music, celebrations, traditional outfits and delicious food for a few days at a stretch. With these fun experiences, kids would have a lot of ideas to write from their experiences. However, young kids may not understand the importance or the reason behind the celebration of Navratri. An essay on Navratri is a great medium for teaching them the significance of the festival, its traditions, and the story behind it. Through essay writing, they will also improve their vocabulary and language skills.
